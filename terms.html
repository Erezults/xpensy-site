2. Description of Service

Xpensy is a mobile application available on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac that helps groups of travelers track and split expenses during trips. The App provides:

  • Expense recording, categorization, tags, and templates
  • Multi-currency support with exchange rate conversion
  • AI-powered receipt scanning (via camera, gallery, documents, or Share Extension)
  • Group expense splitting and settlement summaries
  • Trip organization, folders, and management
  • Receipt gallery and expense search with filters
  • Charts and analytics for trip spending
  • Recurring expense tracking
  • Comments and discussions on expenses
  • Audit log of expense changes
  • Home screen widgets, Apple Watch companion app, Siri Shortcuts, and Dynamic Island integration
  • Push notifications for expense alerts and budget warnings
  • QR code scanning to join trips
  • Offline-first operation with background sync
