Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a trip?

Tap the + button on the home screen and select "New Trip". Enter the trip name, destination, and travel dates, then invite your travel companions by email.

How do I add an expense?

Inside a trip, tap the + button to add an expense manually, or use the AI receipt scanner to automatically extract the amount, date, and category from a photo of your receipt.

How does AI receipt scanning work?

Take a photo of a receipt or import one from your gallery. Xpensy uses Google Gemini to extract the total amount, currency, date, and merchant name — you can review and edit everything before saving.

How does expense splitting work?

Xpensy tracks who paid for each expense and calculates balances automatically. On the trip's Balances screen you can see who owes whom and mark debts as settled.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. Xpensy is fully offline-first — you can add and edit expenses without an internet connection. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

How do I invite someone to my trip?

Open the trip, go to Members, and tap "Invite". Enter their email address. They'll receive an invitation and can join once they create or log into their Xpensy account.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We use Supabase with Row Level Security, so only you and your trip members can access your trip data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data, including trips and expenses. This action cannot be undone. You can also request deletion by emailing support@erezults.com.

The app isn't loading / I'm stuck on a spinner

Force-quit the app and reopen it. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. If you're still stuck after reconnecting, please email us at support@erezults.com with a description of what happened.

I forgot my password

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.
